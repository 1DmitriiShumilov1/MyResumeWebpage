Hello and welcome!

Hello, My name is Dmitrii and currently I am 19 years old. I was born in Perm, Russia in 2001. Before moving to the US, I spent 9 years studying different subjects at school. My 2 favorites were English (as a second language) and Maths. As almost every kid in this world, I did not always like doing the homework, but I still had some temptation to learn, know more. As you can see, I succeeded in learning English. The only thing left for me to do is to succeed in coding.

I moved to the United States with my mother when I was 16. I didn't have very good grades in my Russian school, so I decided that it is time for me to change myself. At August of 2017, I joined Woodcreek High School as a sophomore. 3 years flew by, and I am already a graduate, class of 2020. Throughout these 3 years, I believe I got only one C in one class - and it was English; I managed to get all A's and B's in all of my other classes.
